About the course

INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

Information engineering (IE), also known as information technology engineering (ITE), information engineering methodology (IEM) or data engineering, is a software engineering approach to designing and developing information systems.

Our I.T department, complete with the best software amenities and resources, houses faculty members who are experts in their respective fields. Blockchain, deep learning, AI and cryptography are some of the many extremely poignant disciplines taught in our I.T courses.The ever-changing technology not only impacts the business strategies, product manufacturing, service deliveries but also affects the job roles, expected skills and competencies expected while hiring. Information Technology is rapidly expanding itself to become the ubiquitous branch of engineering and has no domain untouched with the developments in IT. Thus IT engineers are required in all the sectors from coding to large scale Industrial Automation.

PROGRAM EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES (PEO)
Our program will produce graduates who:
PEO 1: Are competent, creative, professionals who would utilize the knowledge gained in the program to serve the need of Society and IT Industry.
PEO 2: Would participate in further professional development by obtaining advance education in IT.
PEO 3: Exhibit awareness about global, ethical, societal, environmental and financial issues as expected from the engineering professionals
PROGRAM SPECIFIC OUTCOME (PSO)
IT engineering graduates will have:
PSO 1: An ability to use and apply current technical concepts and practices in the core information technologies.
PSO 2: An ability to identify and analyze user needs and take them into account in the selection, creation, evaluation and administration of computer-based systems inline with the best practices
PSO 3: An ability to effectively integrate IT-based solutions into the user environment.
